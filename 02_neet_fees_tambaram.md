## H2: Typical NEET Coaching Fee Range in Tambaram (2026)

NEET coaching fees in Tambaram generally fall into three categories:

### H3: Budget Centres (₹30,000 – ₹60,000 per year)
These are smaller, local institutes. Quality varies significantly. Some offer excellent personal attention at low cost. Others cut corners on study material and test frequency. Always check faculty credentials and batch sizes before committing.

### H3: Mid-Range Centres (₹60,000 – ₹1,20,000 per year)
This is where most reputable local coaching centres sit. At this price point, you should expect:
- Qualified subject specialists for all three NEET subjects
- Regular mock tests and performance tracking
- Proper study material included in the fee
- Small to medium batch sizes

### H3: Premium / Franchise Centres (₹1,20,000 – ₹2,00,000+ per year)
Large national brands like Sri Chaitanya and Vedantu operate in this range in Chennai. You pay for brand recognition, infrastructure, and large faculty pools — but batch sizes are often larger and personal attention can be limited.

## H2: What Should Be Included in the Fee?

Before paying any coaching fee in Tambaram, confirm the following is included:

- Physics, Chemistry, and Biology classes
- Study material and notes
- Weekly tests and monthly mock exams
- Doubt-clearing sessions
- Progress reports to parents

If any of these are charged separately, factor that into the total cost before comparing centres.

---

## H2: Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

Some coaching centres in Chennai advertise a base fee but charge extra for:

- Study materials and printed notes
- Mock test series
- Revision batches or crash courses
- Registration or admission fees

Always ask for a complete, all-inclusive fee breakdown in writing before enrolling.

---

## H2: Is Expensive Always Better for NEET?

No. Some of the most expensive centres in Chennai have large batch sizes where individual students receive very little attention. A student struggling with a specific concept in Biology may wait days to get their doubt cleared in a batch of 70 students.

In contrast, a mid-range centre with smaller batches and dedicated faculty can produce significantly better results for average-scoring students — at a fraction of the cost.

The right question is not "which centre is most expensive?" but "which centre will give my child the most attention and structure for their specific level?"
